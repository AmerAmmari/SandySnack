Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

When is the event?

The event will take place on December 25th, 2024, at 123 Sesame Street, Ottawa, ON K1A OB1, Canada.

Can I refund or exchange my tickets?

Tickets are non-refundable past November 25th, 2024. Exchanges may be possible depending on availability.

Are there any age restrictions for the event?

There are no specific age restrictions, but parental guidance is suggested for younger attendees due to mature themes.

How long is the play?

The play will last approximately 2 hours, including a 15-minute intermission.

How can I contact the organizers?

You can contact us via email at info@starsoflebanon.com or by phone at (123) 456-7890.

Can I take pictures or record the performance?

Typically, photography or recording is prohibited during performances, so clarify this rule.
